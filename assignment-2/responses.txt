Briefly describe one way how data travels on the internet.
Data travels the internet through packet switching. Files are broken down into smaller packets that are chronologically in order and are sent on different network routes to their intended destination to be recombined.

What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?
(Domain Name Service) DNS is a way to translate IP addresses to words that are easier to remember for people rather than a list of numbers (IP addresses)

What is a cookie?
A cookie is like an electronic ID, a number for websites/browsers to remember who you are, your preferences and your account.

List the the three basic parts of a URL.
The three basic parts of an URL include the protocol, the domain name, and the path.
