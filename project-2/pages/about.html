About Me

  • Claire Spain is a student (and appreciator of bread) at the University of Montana. She enjoys Media Arts, playing video games and baking.
  • She chose this project as her instructable, as she likes to think of herself as a master at baking challah and she could focus on her web-dev skills, instead of worrying about her recipe.
  • Challah (however, goes by many names) is predominantly used in Sabbath from the religion; Judism. Before the fall of the Temple Rabis requested 1/24 from homebakers and 1/48 from professional bakers to be donated from the dough to the priests. Since the fall of the Temple, same fractions are instead put in foil and burned in fires out of respect. Claire isn't an expert in Judism, but she respects their culture (their delicious bread), and beliefs. More Jewish Cuisine
